The Provision Ground

Climate, decoloniality, and the Black Atlantic. A program of inquiry, its capabilities, and the map that holds it together.

Prepared by Cherryia White for Prof. Michaeline Crichlow. The materials below set out a multiyear research program at the intersection of climate change, decoloniality, and global Blackness, together with the case for the interdisciplinary work it would require and an interactive companion to its geography.

Each piece is open to revision at every level.

01 The Research Prospectus Eight research provocations, an operational agenda, the methods and data, and a publication architecture for the program. → 02 Ground Truth A capabilities brief: what a scholar of the Black Atlantic who is also an ecologist, a diver, and a conservationist brings to the work. → 03 Mapping the Break · The Interactive Atlas A working prototype where every coordinate holds three registers at once: the data, the history, and the testimony. →
